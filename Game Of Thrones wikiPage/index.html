Nine noble families wage war against each other in order to gain control over the mythical land of Westeros. Meanwhile, a force is rising after millenniums and threatens the existence of living men.

Available on Disney+ Hotstar

Casts

Robert Baratheon

PLAYED BY MARK ADDY

King of the Seven Kingdoms of Westeros and longtime friend of Ned Stark. Robert was engaged to Ned's sister, Lyanna, whose abduction by Prince Rhaegar Targaryen incited Robert's uprising against the throne.

Tyrion Lannister

PLAYED BY PETER DINKLAGE

Called "The Imp," because of his small stature, he is the youngest son of Tywin Lannister, the richest lord in the Seven Kingdoms and younger brother to Queen Cersei and Ser Jaime Lannister. What he lacks in height, he makes up in wit.

Cersei Lannister

PLAYED BY LENA HEADEY

Queen of the Seven Kingdoms and wife of King Robert. Fiercely protective of her three children, she also has an unusually tight connection to her twin brother, Jaime.

Catelyn Stark

PLAYED BY MICHELLE FAIRLEY

Wife of Ned Stark, born Catelyn of House Tully, the ruling house of the Riverlands. Originally engaged to Ned’s older brother Brandon, when he was killed, she married Ned.

Jaime Lannister

PLAYED BY NIKOLAJ COSTER-WALDAU

The Queen's twin brother. It was he who murdered the previous King (Aerys), despite having sworn to protect him. This deed earned him his nickname: “Kingslayer.”

Daenerys Targaryen

PLAYED BY EMILIA CLARKE

Princess of House Targaryen, living in exile in Essos with her brother, Viserys.

Viserys Targaryen

PLAYED BY HARRY LLOYD

Prince of House Targaryen, whose father, the "Mad" King Aerys was usurped by King Robert. Viserys lives in self-exile with his sister Daenerys and is obsessed with regaining his family's throne.

Jon Snow

PLAYED BY KIT HARINGTON

Ned's bastard son. He joins the Night’s Watch, following in the footsteps of his Uncle Benjen. His direwolf is named Ghost.

Robb Stark

PLAYED BY RICHARD MADDEN

The oldest of Ned and Cat's five children. His direwolf is named Grey Wind.

Sansa Stark

PLAYED BY SOPHIE TURNER

Ned and Cat's oldest daughter. Demure and ladylike, her dream is to marry Prince Joffrey. Her direwolf is named Lady.

Arya Stark

PLAYED BY MAISIE WILLIAMS

The younger of Ned and Cat's two daughters. A tomboy, she would rather fence than dance. Her direwolf is named Nymeria, after a warrior queen

Bran Stark

PLAYED BY ISAAC HEMPSTEAD-WRIGHT

Ned and Cat's younger son, who has an inquisitive nature and a penchant for climbing Winterfell's walls. His direwolf is named Summer.
